About

Song Composer & Singer


Darshan Raval is an Indian singer, composer, actor, and songwriter. He sings predominantly in Hindi along with Gujarati and Telugu. He was the first runner-up in the StarPlus music reality show, India's Raw Star in 2014.




Origin & Childhood



Darshan Raval was born in a Hindu family on 18th October 1994 in Ahmedabad, Gujarat..Darshan Raval had more interest in singing since childhood in comparison to studies. That's why he wanted to be a singer, but his father, on the other hand, wanted him to study engineering. Darshan has studied in Shri Swami Narayan Gurukul, Ahmedabad.


"Be You. If people like it, great.My music is honest, and it comes from the heart. I have never done anything to become famous."
